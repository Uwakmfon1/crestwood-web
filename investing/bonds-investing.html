Cash Reserve Information

The 4.50% APY on Cash Reserve represents the weighted average of participating Program Banks and is current as of September 20, 2024. This rate is variable and subject to daily changes. A minimum deposit of $10 is required with no minimum balance requirement.

FDIC Insurance: Deposits are eligible for up to $2,000,000 in FDIC insurance for individual accounts ($4,000,000 for joint accounts) across up to eight Program Banks. Clients are responsible for monitoring total assets at each bank to ensure FDIC limits aren't exceeded.
